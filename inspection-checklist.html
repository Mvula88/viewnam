5. Test Drive Assessment

Test Drive / Full Package

Drive at least 10-15 km covering urban and highway conditions where possible. Test all gears and braking scenarios.

Inspection Point G F P N/A Photo Notes
Engine & Power
Acceleration
Smooth power delivery, no hesitation or misfires
Turbo (if applicable)
Boost response, lag, wastegate sounds
Engine noise under load
Knocking, tapping, whining at higher RPM
Exhaust smoke under acceleration
Smoke colour and amount when pushing throttle
Transmission
Gear changes (auto)
Smooth shifts, no jerking, no slipping, no delay
Clutch (manual)
Bite point, slipping under load, judder
Gear selection (manual)
All gears engage smoothly, no crunching
Reverse gear
Engages cleanly, no grinding
4x4 system (if applicable)
Engages and disengages, diff lock, low range
Steering, Suspension & Brakes
Steering response
Sharp, not vague, no excessive play
Vehicle pulls left/right
Hands-off straight-line test (safe conditions)
Steering wheel vibration
At speed — wheel balance. Under braking — warped discs
Suspension over bumps
Clunking, excessive bouncing, bottoming out
Braking performance
Stops straight, no grinding, pedal feel firm
ABS activation
Pedal pulsation on hard braking (normal)
General Driving Impressions
Road noise
Excessive wind or tyre noise, wheel bearing hum
Unusual rattles or squeaks
Dashboard, doors, undercarriage
Cruise control (if fitted)
Engages, holds speed, disengages properly
Warning lights during drive
Any dashboard warnings that appear while driving

6. Required Photo Checklist

Exterior — Minimum Required

  • Front view (straight on)
  • Rear view (straight on)
  • Driver side (full length)
  • Passenger side (full length)
  • Front 3/4 angle (driver side)
  • Rear 3/4 angle (passenger side)
  • Roof (from above or angled)
  • Each wheel/tyre close-up (x4)
  • Windscreen condition
  • Headlights close-up (both)
  • Tail lights close-up (both)
  • All body damage / defects found

Interior — Minimum Required

  • Dashboard (full view)
  • Instrument cluster / odometer
  • Driver seat
  • Rear seats
  • Steering wheel
  • Infotainment / radio
  • Boot / cargo area
  • Spare wheel well
  • Pedals (wear check)
  • Service book pages (if available)
  • Keys (how many)
  • All interior defects found

Mechanical — Minimum Required

  • Engine bay (full view)
  • Oil dipstick close-up
  • Coolant reservoir
  • Battery
  • Belts & hoses
  • Undercarriage (from front)
  • Exhaust system
  • Any leaks or damage found
  • VIN plate
  • OBD scanner screen (all codes)
