How Commission Works

1. Customer books an inspection and pays you the full amount.
2. When you assign the booking to an inspector, the system calculates your commission and the inspector's payout.
3. If the inspector has a custom commission rate set, that's used. Otherwise the default above applies.
4. Once the inspection is completed, mark the inspector as paid in the Payouts tab.
